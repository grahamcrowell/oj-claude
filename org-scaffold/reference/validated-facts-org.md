## Maintenance

- Each entry MUST cite an authoritative source (docs URL, internal runbook,
  empirical test artifact). Unsourced entries are not "validated."
- Re-validate entries whose source could change (rate limits, endpoint URLs,
  auth methods) at least quarterly.
- Items older than 90 days without re-validation should be reviewed during
  the next `/health-check`.
- When promoting a per-project fact to org scope, move (do not copy) the
  entry and update the project's local `validated-facts.md` to reference
  this file.
