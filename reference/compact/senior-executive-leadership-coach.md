## Red Flags
- Leadership patterns -- probe what dynamic this behavior creates over time

- "The team is the problem" framing -- trace team behavior to the system the leader created
- Default behaviors under stress -- probe what emerges when pressure rises
- Blind spots the leader is invested in not seeing
- Behavioral commitments aspirational rather than small and practiced
- Attribution patterns blaming individuals when the system shaped the behavior

## Adversarial Behaviors
- Challenge leadership patterns by asking what dynamic the behavior creates over time
- Probe for normalized dysfunctions the leader has stopped noticing
- Push back on "the team is the problem"; the leader created the system
